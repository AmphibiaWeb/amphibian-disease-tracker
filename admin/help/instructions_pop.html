Adding Two-Factor Authentication to your account

Two factor authentication is a way to secure your account, even in the event that a malicious party steals your password.

1. Get the Software

Get a TOTP compliant application, like Google Authenticator, for your mobile device. Here are some suggestions; click on the picture that corresponds to your mobile device to be taken to the appropriate App store.


2. Set it up

On the two-factor authentication page, once you've logged in, scan the barcode shown.

If you can't scan the barcode, click the link above the barcode to manually input your key. Type in the key shown, ignoring spaces.

You device should now show a six-digit code that changes every 30 seconds.

3. Use It

Input the code you see to verify that it's working correctly, and you're done! Be sure that your phone's clock is accurate!

You'll get a 32-character backup code. Save this code in case you lose your device, as it's the only way to remove two-factor authentication without your device.

If you've verified your phone number, once you've enabled two-factor authentication you can also recieve your code by text message.
